Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add **onWhisper** to my server?

Simply go to our Add to Discord page and follow the instructions to invite **onWhisper** to your server.

How do I set up moderation commands?

Once the bot is added to your server, use the `!setup` command to begin configuring the moderation tools. You can find more details in the setup guide.

What do I do if the bot isn't responding?

Ensure that the bot has the necessary permissions in your server. You can also try restarting the bot with the `!restart` command. If the issue persists, contact support.
